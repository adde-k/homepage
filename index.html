About Us

Sabroso Travels started out as a conversation between our team members Marcus and Annika when they were planning their first trip to Cuba. They found that informational resources online were scarce so they turned to what would later become the second half of the team, Emelie and Adiba, for their best Cuba tips. On the morning of Marcus and Annikas last day in Cuba, when having coffe on the balcony of a small casa particular just a few minutes from the infamous Obispo street, they decided that they would make reality of the conversation they had earlier and that is how Sabroso Travels came to be.

Sabroso Travels have been running successful operations since 2018 and keeps on growing. We take great pride in our sustainibility focus, making sure the local businesses we collaborate with are sutained through a longer period of time. This brings financial and social security to the locals who work with us. When you are traveling with us nor only do you guarantee the time of ypur life but also you help guarantee a more stable future for the Cuban community.
